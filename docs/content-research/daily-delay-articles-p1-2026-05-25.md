## Editorial decisions

- Public copy avoids competitor names and drafting-process language.
- Each article links naturally to `/naknada-za-kasnjenje-leta` and `/en/flight-delay-compensation`.
- The batch avoids duplicating existing per-airline and per-duration pages, and fills operational-reason/evidence gaps in the same primary parent.
- All topics are treated as fact-sensitive scenarios: the public copy does not overclaim compensation when security, weather, ATC or airport-wide limits may be extraordinary circumstances.
- The automation angle is the repeated case-file structure: arrival, route, reason, responsibility, care, receipts and response status.
