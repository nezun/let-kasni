## Scope

This note covers six detailed operational-scenario articles added under the
flight-delay main guide. They deepen the flight-delay silo without duplicating
the existing airline, duration, Serbia/EU route, or airport-action pages.

## Approved outline logic

Every H2 serves one of the P1 reasons: direct scenario search intent, a legal or
eligibility condition, or a source-supported user step. The articles use the same
decision model for repeatability: covered route, final arrival, reason, care,
rerouting and evidence file.

## Visual/checklist modules

Each article includes a route table-intent section and an evidence checklist.
Runtime article visuals add the case-file and professional-review modules, while
